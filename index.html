Chatting Tips

  • Make conversations feel natural.
  • Observe before asking questions.
  • Reference previous conversations.
  • Focus on curiosity instead of interrogation.
  • Mirror the fan's energy without copying it.
  • Create curiosity before offering content.
  • Never open with prices.
  • Make premium content feel exclusive.
  • Talk like a real person.
  • Don't disappear after a purchase.
  • Remember details.
  • Ask about previous topics.
  • Celebrate birthdays.
  • Check in randomly.
  • Be consistent.
